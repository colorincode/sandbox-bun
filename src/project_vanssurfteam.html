The “Can't Steal Our Vibe” documentary film follows a grassroots initiative unifying two cultures worlds apart through a common love of wave riding.

The Gudauskas brothers presented the 3rd Annual Stoke-O-Rama surf contest at T-Street in San Clemente.

Positivity is the key to making this world a better place. The Positive Vibe Warriors, an organization founded by the Gudauskas brothers, has been working to pass the positivity to the entire world. Through their own initiatives and by partnering with other like-minded groups from around the globe, the boys have made the choice to share the stoke with the entire world and are providing support and relief to people in need through surfing, environmental preservation and ocean education.

Get involved. Pass the stoke at positivevibewarriors.com
